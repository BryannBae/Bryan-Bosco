About BikeFever

Founded in 2020 by Bryan Bosco Ng, a college student from UIB 2SIMD Information Systems, BikeFever was born from a passion for cycling and a vision to bring quality bikes and exceptional service to the local community. What started as a small venture has quickly grown into a trusted destination for biking enthusiasts of all levels. At BikeFever, we believe that the right bike can spark adventure, promote a healthy lifestyle, and connect people in powerful ways. Whether you’re a casual rider or a seasoned cyclist, our team is here to help you find the perfect fit — from bikes and gear to maintenance and advice. We’re more than just a bike shop — we’re a community fueled by a love for two wheels. Join the fever. Ride with us.

We partner with the best manufacturers to bring you the latest in cycling technology and design.
